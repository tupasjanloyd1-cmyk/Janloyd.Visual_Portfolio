Under @janloyd.visual, I specialize in Minimalist Cinematic and Quiet Luxury post-production, engineered specifically for the real estate market. Every node, every grade, every audio layer is intentional — designed to elevate a property from listing to landmark.

I work as a dedicated remote specialist — which means you get agency-level fidelity with boutique-level focus, faster turnaround, and a curator who treats your brand as their own.

What I Deliver

Scope of
Craft

01
Advanced Color Grading
Implementation of a "Nordic Luxury" palette — clean whites, neutralized yellow light contamination, and preserved exterior dynamic range. Transforming flat log profiles into high-contrast cinematic narratives using advanced node structures and custom LUTs in DaVinci Resolve.
02
Immersive Sound Design
Tactile audio engineering that enhances the perceived weight of materials — marble, glass, hardwood. Atmospheric reverb creates a sense of space and breathability in large architectural rooms. Every sonic layer is curated to match the visual luxury of the property.
03
Omni-Channel Optimization
The Cinematic Feature: 4K horizontal for YouTube and high-end presentations. The High-Velocity Reel: 9:16 vertical edits engineered for Instagram and TikTok engagement. Plus custom-graded hero stills for maximum click-through on listing thumbnails.
